# Metro Point Technology Logo Design

## Overview
The Metro Point Technology logo is designed to represent our identity as a technology company built by insurance professionals, while maintaining visual brand consistency with Metro Point Insurance.

## Logo Elements

### 1. Golden Sun (Top)
**Visual**: Circular gradient sun in gold (#D4AF37 to #E5C158)

**Symbolism**:
- **New Beginnings**: Represents innovation and fresh solutions in insurance technology
- **Warmth & Growth**: Symbolizes our nurturing approach to client relationships
- **Illumination**: Reflects our mission to bring clarity and insight to complex insurance operations

### 2. Three Server Blocks (Center)
**Visual**: Three navy blue (#003B5C, #002940) rectangular server/data center blocks with varying heights

**Symbolism**:
- **Technology Infrastructure**: Represents our core business of building software solutions
- **Stability & Security**: Solid blocks convey reliability and data protection
- **Scalability**: Varying heights suggest growth and adaptability
- **Three Pillars**: Represents our three main product offerings:
  1. Left block: Commission Tracker (for individual agents)
  2. Center block (tallest): AMS Platform (our flagship enterprise solution)
  3. Right block: Agent CRM & WRAP Generator (supporting tools)

**Blinking Gold Lights**:
- **Active Systems**: Animated lights simulate real server activity, showing we're live and operational
- **24/7 Availability**: Continuous blinking represents always-on service
- **Data Processing**: Each light represents data flowing through our systems
- **Gold Color**: Ties back to our premium service quality and the golden sun above

### 3. Golden Swoosh (Bottom)
**Visual**: Curved horizontal line that dips down in the middle, forming a gentle arc

**Symbolism**:
- **Horizon Line**: Represents a sunrise over a landscape, suggesting new opportunities
- **Foundation**: Grounds the design, symbolizing our stable foundation in insurance expertise
- **Connection**: The flowing curve suggests connectivity and integration between systems
- **Brand Continuity**: Directly borrowed from Metro Point Insurance logo to maintain family branding

## Brand Relationship

### Metro Point Family Identity
Both Metro Point Insurance and Metro Point Technology share:
- **Golden sun** at the top (warmth, growth, illumination)
- **Golden swoosh** at the bottom (horizon, foundation)
- **Navy blue** primary color (trust, professionalism, stability)
- **Gold accent** color (premium quality, value)

### Differentiation
- **Insurance Logo**: Features buildings (representing the agency's physical presence and client protection)
- **Technology Logo**: Features servers (representing software infrastructure and digital solutions)

## Logo Variations

### 1. logo-animated.svg
- Full logo with animated blinking server lights
- Used on website navbar for dynamic visual interest
- 9 independent CSS animations with varying timing (1.5s - 2.5s)
- Each light fades between 100% and 30% opacity

### 2. logo.svg
- Static version without animations
- Used for print materials, email signatures, documents
- Identical design without CSS keyframe animations

### 3. favicon.svg
- Simplified icon-only version (100x100px)
- Contains only the graphic elements (sun, servers, swoosh)
- No text - optimized for browser tabs and mobile bookmarks

## Design Philosophy

### Built by Agents, for Agents
Our logo reflects that we're not just a technology company - we're insurance professionals who build technology. The combination of traditional insurance branding (navy/gold, serif fonts, professional aesthetic) with modern tech elements (servers, animations, digital infrastructure) perfectly captures our unique position in the market.

### Visual Continuity
By maintaining consistent branding with Metro Point Insurance, we signal to clients that:
1. We share the same values and expertise
2. We understand insurance operations deeply
3. Our technology is built on 30+ years of real-world experience
4. We're a trusted extension of the Metro Point family

### Modern Yet Timeless
The animated server lights add contemporary flair while the classic color scheme and serif typography ensure the logo won't feel dated in years to come.

## Usage Guidelines

### Primary Use
- Website header (animated version)
- Digital presentations
- Email signatures (static version)

### Print Use
- Business cards (static version)
- Letterhead (static version)
- Marketing materials (static version)

### Icon Use
- Browser favicon
- Mobile app icons
- Social media profile pictures

### Minimum Size
- Full logo: 200px width minimum
- Icon only: 32px x 32px minimum

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of one server block on all sides.

### Do Not
- Change the color scheme
- Rearrange logo elements
- Add effects or filters
- Stretch or distort proportions
- Place on busy backgrounds that reduce readability

## Technical Specifications

### File Format
SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics)

**Benefits**:
- Infinite scalability without quality loss
- Small file size
- Supports animation (CSS keyframes)
- Web-optimized
- Accessible and SEO-friendly

### Dimensions
- Full Logo: 400px x 120px (viewBox)
- Favicon: 100px x 100px (viewBox)

### Animation Details
```css
9 unique keyframe animations
Animation duration: 1.5s - 2.5s (varied)
Animation type: Infinite loop
Effect: Opacity fade (100% to 30%)
Purpose: Simulate real server activity lights
```
